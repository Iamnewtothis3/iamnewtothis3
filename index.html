Reasons why Dogs are great!

Read time 7 minutes

They are great for mental and physical health

"They are amazing..." - Safo, 2024

They help with downtime just like a home full of kids, dogs help you relax and be in the present moment. Their needs such as regular exercise and playtime require us to focus our attention on them, hence forcing us to truly be in the monet and relax.

They are awesome companions

"...They are amazing companions" - Safo, 2024

They are great companions because they are loving, loyal and affectionate. They are great sources of support by bring joy to households and reducing streaa and lonliness. Due to their amazing nature they are often considered part of the family.
